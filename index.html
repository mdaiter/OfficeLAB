The big picture.

We want to give individuals the ability to create a modular space that works for them, allowing them to get more work done the way they want to. Not everyone works the same. We understand that. The place where you work shouldn't encourage uniformity and uncomfortable, impersonal environments.

With our new office space, you'll be begging to get more work done. Once you neter the cubicle, you'll still be able to see out and around you, giving you an open experience. However, you'll also be able to listen to music, work while watching a movie, or enstill complete silence within your work dojo. The cubicle adapts to your workstyle through blocking noise while allowing an individual to look onto and around the world. The auditory distractions while working in an open office space disappear without the impersonability of a traditional cubicle.

Attributes of the system

See why our cubicle's bitchin'.

Passive Noise Cancellation

Have that annoying co-worker sit right next to you? Now you don't have to use the Xanax to deal with your problems! With our passive noise cancelling system, two thick sheets of glass wall you off from the world.

Movable Desk Screen

Ever need that big screen, but didn't want to move from your chair to access it? Ever want that desk, but had too many distractions on your big screen to use it properly (get off of Facebook!)? Now you can have both, with the ability to shift your big screen to or from your view with the flick of the wrist!

Shiftable Glass Walls

Want to close yourself off from the world, because of the people sitting next to you? Like the guy to your right, but not the girl to your left? Don't want to talk to your boss? Now, you can shift glass walls around you to isolate yourself from the world!
